Register your spherene account

Welcome to the Spherene world!

The first step to land on Spherene is to have your own Spherene account.

If you are not a student or educator, You can register your Spherene account here, and start to enjoy your 10-days trial journey.

Meanwhile, we offer Free License for students and special discounts for educators. Check this page and register yourself there.

After you submit the registration form, an email to set the password of your account will be sent to your email address within 5 minutes. If you did not receive it, please double check your spam or junk mailbox before you contact our support team.

Download & Installation

You can login the dashboard of your spherene account in this link, or click in the login button in the top-right corner of our website:

In the dashboard, by clicking the button with nTop logo, you can download the installers of Spherene:

Once downloaded, double click the installer to run and install the Spherene. Please make sure the internet is connected and nTop is closed during installation.

After installation, you should see the Spherene block in the Connector section. There will be no Connector section in nTop before you install Spherene or any other connectors. If you did not see the Connector section after installation and restarting nTop, please double check whether you have enabled Connector in nTop setting -> Ribbons.

Once you finish the installation, you can start to play with Spherene following Quickstart First Time Use.

For detailed understanding of how to use spherene, you can download the examples from Resources and find detail descriptions in the tutorial.

Uninstallation​

To uninstall the Spherene connector, please execute the same .msi file to begin the uninstallation process.

System Requirements

  • Internet connection
  • Windows 10+ / MacOS X 14+, 32GB RAM
